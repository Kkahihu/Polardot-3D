About Us

About the product

Polardot 3D Survey Pro is a cloud-based software that integrates a variety of construction data such as:

  • Drone images.
  • Drone survey mapping products.
  • Lidar data.
  • GPS/survey equipment data.
  • 2D/3D CAD design or as built data.
  • Architectural 3D models.

Services

Uses in the construction lifecycle

3D/2D Surveying, Terrain Mapping & 3D site reconstruction.

Construction Building & Infrastructure deviation and issue tracking.

Construction site asset management.

Construction, Building & Infrastructure asset inspection and progress tracking.

Central remote monitoring and site visualization on the cloud.

Earthwork estimations and stockpile volumetric analysis.

Transmission Line Corridor vegetation management.

Highlights

Some of the highlights with the product

  • Accurately measure volume, areas, heights, points and distances using survey grade accuracy.
  • Evaluate construction progress and validate work with Earthworks analysis.
  • Quantify stockpile material inventory.
  • Analyze construction deviation.
  • 3D Cross-section analysis, plotting and export.
  • Export measurements (points, distances,areas and volumes) in common GIS and CAD formats.

  • Conduct detailed inspection and analysis from high-resolution imagery in a fraction of the time, reducing downtime.
  • Inspect hard-to-reach areas without putting your team in danger.

  • Compare as-built to as-designed with design plan overlays.
  • Compare different data sets to gather rich insights over time.
  • Visualize contours,2D as built data, 3D meshes, pointclouds, orthomosaic maps and digital elevation models in one platform.
